Corporate Overview

New Brick Industries Pvt. Ltd. is a premier brick manufacturing company in Northern India, supplying top-quality bricks to major cities including Noida, Delhi, Gurugram, Lucknow, Jaipur, Faridabad, Kanpur, and regions in Haryana, Uttar Pradesh, Rajasthan, and Madhya Pradesh.

Our Products & Services

  • Red clay bricks for traditional construction
  • Fly ash bricks for eco-friendly projects
  • Concrete bricks for heavy-duty applications
  • Custom brick designs for architectural projects
  • Bulk supply and logistics support for large projects

We are an ISO 9001:2015 | ISO 14001:2015 certified company, committed to sustainable manufacturing practices and delivering superior quality bricks. Our advanced production facilities ensure timely delivery and consistency, making us a trusted partner for builders in Delhi, Noida, Gurugram, Faridabad, Lucknow, Jaipur, Kanpur, Haryana, Rajasthan, MP & Uttar Pradesh.

BrickMaster Industries stands out as one of the most reliable brick suppliers in the region, with a strong presence in both urban and industrial markets. Our dedication to quality and innovation has positioned us among the top brick manufacturers in Northern India.
